1. Personal Information We Collect

This section describes the categories of Personal Information we collect and the categories of third parties with which we share the Personal Information.

Prospective Customers: We may collect interest and demographic information for marketing purposes, using third parties who supply information about prospective customers, such as lead buy partners and data brokers.

All Users of the Sites ("Users"): We may collect device, usage, and activity information, as well as non-precise location information for security, system administration, marketing, and improvement purposes, in order to provide the Sites to Users. Data is collected by analytics providers and indirectly by observing Users' activities and behavior while using the Sites, and may be stored by third-party analytics providers.

Users Whose Activities Express Interest in Our Products or Services: We may collect interest and demographic information for marketing purposes. Data is collected by analytics providers and indirectly by observing Users' activities and behavior while using the Sites.

Users and Other Individuals Who Apply for a Financial Product or Service, Including a Mortgage: We collect the following information: name and contact information, user-selected credentials (email address and password), business affiliation and information for affiliated business, application information including purpose of application, guarantor information, residency information, asset information, and liquidity information, financial information such as bank statements or other banking information, credit information, and identification. We do so to determine your eligibility for the requested financial product or service, to verify your identity and business affiliation, and for customer service purposes. Data is collected directly from Users, indirectly from agents of individuals seeking to apply for financial products or services on such individuals' behalf, from third parties that provide information about creditworthiness, and from third parties that verify User-provided information. This information may be shared with third parties that provide and/or compile information about creditworthiness, such as credit bureaus and consumer reporting agencies, and with our institutional lending partners.

Users Who Pay an Appraisal Deposit in Connection With Their Application for a Financial Product or Service: We collect information about the payment transaction to confirm receipt of the deposit from our payment processing partner.

Users Who Provide Feedback or Send Us a Message Using the Sites: Names and contact information, along with the contents of messages, may be collected in order to review, respond, and take responsive action, to improve our business and the Sites. This is collected directly from Users.

Users Who Apply for Employment With Us: We collect the following information: name and contact information, professional qualification information including work experience, educational credentials, licenses, certifications, and references, work eligibility information, and equal employment information, in order to evaluate the application for employment. This information may be collected directly from Users and from third parties who verify professional qualification information.
